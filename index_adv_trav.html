Welcome to the Advantage Conference 2016

On behalf of the Advantage Travel Partnership team and board of directors, I would like to extend a very warm welcome to you for joining us on board MSC Fantasia for the 2016 Advantage Conference.

As always, we do recognise the effort many of you have put in to be with us on this exciting conference cruise and I can assure you that we will offer you all a programme that will more than justify your time and cost investment. Although some of you will have the benefit of experiencing a cruise for the very first time, we expect everyone to derive real value from the overall conference experience.

Echoing the conference theme of Blink, the business programme will place a strong emphasis on understanding the scale, pace and nature of the inevitable changes that we will all face in the future. However, we intend to go well beyond charting change and will offer a range of solutions to allow our members and business partners to thrive in this changing environment. Our speakers will be thought-provoking and the business sessions will be tailored to both a leisure and business travel audience.

As far as the social programme is concerned, we are delighted to incorporate our conference into a cruise and we intend to take full advantage of what MSC Fantasia and the Mediterranean destinations en route have to offer our delegates.

So please do enjoy the next few days and I can confidently promise you a voyage of exciting discovery, offering you great business potential to take back to your individual businesses.

Very kind regards

Julia Lo Bue-Said
Managing Director
Advantage Travel Partnership
