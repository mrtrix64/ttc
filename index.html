App Sponsor

Home

intuitive is a dynamic and progressive travel technology company based in South London. Travel is at the heart of what we do, so we are excited to be sponsoring The Travel Convention this year and thereby displaying our commitment and support of the travel industry.

Our flagship product iVector is a powerful end-to-end reservation platform for tour operators, OTAs and accommodation wholesalers. The system delivers many benefits to enable our customers to succeed and grow in a competitive travel market, tailor made and dynamically packaging tour operators will be delighted with the available tools to support their business.

iVector provides the most comprehensive access to product

  • Over 90 accommodation connections, so you can sell more rooms to more people.
  • Connections to search and book flights – Amadeus, Galileo, Worldspan, Multicom, Ypsilon, EasyJet
  • Connections for an extensive range of product – attractions, tours, payment gateways etc
  • XML connections to increase and enhance sales channels and provide easy connectivity for your selling partners.
  • An impressive suite of online tools to make your fulfilment process a whole lot simpler. Automated workflows will improve efficiency and save you time and money.
  • A fully outsourced solution in the cloud, delivering entirely scalable solutions and reducing infrastructure costs.

Intuitive have done extensive development to deliver a high performing efficient reservation platform for the dynamic packaging and tailor made tour operator.  Package search, blended flight search, price match, advanced occupancy, comprehensive enquiry management/quoting capability, pre-packaging, complex itinerary support, group bookings all make the solution an ideal reservation platform for a growing business.  Add this to an excellent call centre based “inspire me” search supporting your sales team to excite their customers’ makes iVector the ideal tool for your business

intuitive and iVector deliver outstanding functionality for some of the UK’s leading travel companies.

By sponsoring the app our goal is to support technology to enrich your experience at the convention, leave the paperwork behind and allow the app to keep you updated on what’s coming next or reminding you where you should be and when.

ABTA

Home

ABTA has been a trusted travel brand for over 65 years. Our purpose is to help our Members to grow their businesses successfully and sustainably, and to help their customers travel with confidence.

The ABTA brand stands for support, protection and expertise. This means consumers have confidence in ABTA and a strong trust in ABTA Members. These qualities are core to us as they ensure that holidaymakers remain confident in the holiday products that they buy from our Members.

We help our Members and their customers navigate through today's changing travel landscape by raising standards in the industry; offering schemes of financial protection; providing an independent complaints resolution service should something go wrong; giving guidance on issues from sustainability to health and safety and by presenting a united voice to government to ensure the industry and the public get a fair deal.

ABTA currently has around 1,200 Members, with a combined annual UK turnover of £32.07 billion. For more details about what we do, what being an ABTA Member means and how we help the British public travel with confidence.
